Our Story

A sanctuary carved
from coral and light

Established in 2009 on the most storied stretch of Sri Lanka's southern coastline, Ocean View Resort has grown from a boutique family guesthouse into one of the Indian Ocean's most celebrated coastal retreats.

Every room faces the water. Every meal uses ingredients grown within forty kilometres. Every experience is quietly extraordinary — whether you spend your days swimming in the infinity pool, exploring colonial-era Galle Fort, or simply reading in a hammock as the Indian Ocean breathes beneath you.
