Help & System Guidelines
HelpUI → User: displayGuidelines()

📋 Bookings / Live Reservations

  • Confirm Check-in — marks guest as Checked In, room becomes Occupied. A toast confirmation email notification appears.
  • Process Check-out — marks guest as Checked Out, room returns to Available.
  • Download Invoice — generates a branded PDF invoice for the guest.
  • New Reservation — fill all fields; the system checks for room conflicts automatically.

🏠 Room Management

  • Rooms: Available, Reserved, Occupied, Maintenance.
  • Maintenance rooms cannot be booked — excluded from reservation dropdown.
  • Status syncs automatically on check-in / check-out / cancellation.

👥 Staff Accounts (Admin Only)

  • Create — fill all fields, click Create Account → StaffDB.save.
  • Edit — click ✏️ to update name, email, role, status, or password.
  • Delete — click 🗑, confirm the deletion prompt → StaffDB.delete.

💰 Billing (Sequence 4)

  • Enter a reservation number → BillingService.calculateBill → shows nights, rate, VAT (12%), total.
  • Click Print Bill (browser print) → printBill(resNo) → printSuccess().
  • Click Download PDF for a branded invoice.

🌙/☀️ Theme Toggle

Click the sun/moon icon in the top-right to switch between Dark "Midnight Abyss" and Light "Luminous Ocean". Preference persists in localStorage.

⏏ Logout / Exit

App.exit() → App.saveAndClose() → session cleared → redirect to login. Click the Logout button at the bottom of the sidebar.
